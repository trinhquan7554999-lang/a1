Shipping Policy

  1. Home
  2. Shipping Policy

Last Updated: August 15, 2025

At Easy Tape, we strive to provide reliable and efficient shipping services to deliver our premium measuring tapes to your doorstep. This Shipping Policy outlines the shipping methods, delivery times, costs, and other important information regarding the delivery of your purchases.

1. Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to the following locations:

  • United Kingdom (including Northern Ireland)
  • European Union countries
  • United States and Canada
  • Australia and New Zealand
  • Selected countries in Asia (Japan, Singapore, South Korea, and Hong Kong)

If your country is not listed above and you would like to place an order, please contact us to discuss shipping options.

2. Shipping Methods and Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping (UK): 2-3 business days
  • Express Shipping (UK): 1-2 business days
  • Standard Shipping (EU): 4-7 business days
  • Standard Shipping (US and Canada): 5-10 business days
  • Standard Shipping (Australia and New Zealand): 7-14 business days
  • Standard Shipping (Asia): 7-14 business days
  • Express International: 3-5 business days (available for most destinations)

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary depending on customs clearance procedures, local postal services, and other factors beyond our control.

3. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location, the shipping method selected, and the weight of your order. The exact shipping cost will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

As a general guide, here are our standard shipping rates:

  • UK Standard Shipping: £2.99 (Free for orders over £30)
  • UK Express Shipping: £4.99
  • EU Standard Shipping: £5.99 - £9.99 (Free for orders over £50)
  • US and Canada Standard Shipping: £7.99 - £12.99 (Free for orders over £75)
  • Australia and New Zealand Standard Shipping: £9.99 - £14.99
  • Asia Standard Shipping: £9.99 - £14.99
  • Express International: £14.99 - £19.99

4. Order Processing

All orders are processed and dispatched from our warehouse in the UK. Here's our typical order processing timeline:

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM (GMT) on business days (Monday to Friday) are typically processed and dispatched the same day.
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM (GMT) or on weekends and holidays will be processed on the next business day.

During peak periods (such as holidays), order processing may take 1-2 additional business days.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive a confirmation email with your tracking information. You can use this tracking number to monitor the progress of your delivery on the courier's website.

For any questions regarding your shipment status, please contact our customer service team with your order number and we will be happy to assist you.

6. Customs, Duties, and Taxes

For international orders, please note that you may be responsible for paying customs duties, taxes, and fees levied by your country's customs authority. These charges are not included in the purchase price or shipping costs and are the responsibility of the recipient.

Easy Tape has no control over these charges and cannot predict what they may be. Customs policies vary widely from country to country. For more information, please contact your local customs office.

7. Delivery Issues

In the event of any delivery issues such as:

  • Lost or damaged packages
  • Significant delays beyond the estimated delivery time
  • Incorrect delivery address
  • Missing items from your order

Please contact us as soon as possible with your order number and details of the issue. We will work with the courier service to resolve the problem promptly.

8. Returns Shipping

For information about returning products, including who pays for the return shipping costs, please refer to our Refund Policy.

9. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Shipping Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our shipping practices.

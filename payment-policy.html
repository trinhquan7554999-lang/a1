Last Updated: August 15, 2025

This Payment Policy outlines the terms and conditions for making payments on the Easy Tape website. By making a purchase on our website, you agree to comply with and be bound by this policy.

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods for purchases made on our website:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay
  • Bank Transfers: Available for select countries
  • Gift Cards: Easy Tape gift cards and e-gift certificates

The availability of certain payment methods may vary depending on your geographic location.

2. Payment Security

Protecting your payment information is our top priority. We implement the following security measures to ensure safe transactions:

  • Our website uses SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption technology to protect your personal and payment information.
  • We comply with PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard) requirements to ensure secure handling of credit card information.
  • We do not store complete credit card information on our servers. Card details are securely processed through our payment service providers.
  • For PayPal and other digital wallet payments, you are redirected to the provider's secure payment gateway.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Any information you provide is done so at your own risk.

3. Pricing and Currency

Our pricing and currency policies are as follows:

  • All prices listed on our website are in British Pounds (£) unless otherwise specified.
  • For customers outside the United Kingdom, approximate prices in your local currency may be displayed for convenience, but the final charge will be in British Pounds.
  • Your payment provider or bank will convert the amount to your local currency at their current exchange rate and may charge a conversion fee.
  • Prices include UK Value Added Tax (VAT) where applicable. For international orders, additional taxes or duties may be charged upon delivery, which are the customer's responsibility.

We reserve the right to change prices at any time without prior notice. However, orders already placed will not be affected by subsequent price changes.

4. Billing Information

When placing an order, you agree to provide accurate and complete billing information:

  • The billing address provided must match the address associated with your payment method.
  • Orders may be subject to verification checks to prevent fraudulent transactions.
  • In case of discrepancies in billing information, we reserve the right to request additional verification or cancel the order.

For security reasons, we may contact you to verify your payment information before processing your order.

5. Order Processing

Our order processing procedure is as follows:

  • When you place an order, your payment method will be authorized for the full amount.
  • Your payment will be captured (charged) when your order is processed for shipping.
  • For pre-order or back-ordered items, your payment method may be authorized at the time of order but will only be charged when the item is ready to ship.
  • You will receive an order confirmation email once your order is placed and a payment confirmation email once the payment is processed.

Please note that it may take 1-2 business days for the charge to appear on your statement, depending on your payment provider.

6. Payment Failures and Declined Transactions

In case of payment failures or declined transactions:

  • If your payment is declined, you will receive a notification with general information about why the transaction failed.
  • For privacy and security reasons, we may not receive specific details about why your card was declined. Please contact your payment provider for more information.
  • You may attempt to place your order again using the same or a different payment method.
  • If you continue to experience payment issues, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

Orders with failed payments will not be processed or shipped until a successful payment is made.

7. Discounts and Promotions

Our policy on discounts and promotional offers:

  • Discount codes must be entered during the checkout process before completing the payment.
  • Most promotional codes cannot be applied retroactively to orders already placed.
  • Unless otherwise specified, discount codes cannot be combined with other promotions or offers.
  • Discount codes may have expiration dates, minimum purchase requirements, or other limitations as specified in the promotion terms.
  • We reserve the right to modify or cancel any promotion without prior notice.

If you believe a discount code was not properly applied to your order, please contact our customer service team within 24 hours of placing your order.

8. Gift Cards

Easy Tape gift cards are subject to the following terms:

  • Gift cards can be redeemed for merchandise on our website by entering the gift card code during checkout.
  • Gift cards have no expiration date and do not incur any fees.
  • Gift cards cannot be redeemed for cash unless required by law.
  • If your purchase total is less than the value of your gift card, the remaining balance will be available for future purchases.
  • If your purchase total exceeds the value of your gift card, you must pay the difference using another payment method.

Lost or stolen gift cards may be replaced with proof of purchase and at our discretion.

9. Fraud Prevention

To protect our customers and business from fraud:

  • We may implement fraud detection systems and procedures to verify orders.
  • Orders flagged by our fraud detection system may require additional verification or may be canceled.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service, terminate accounts, or cancel orders at our discretion, particularly in cases of suspected fraudulent activity.
  • Using the website to engage in fraudulent activity is strictly prohibited and may result in legal action.

If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Payment Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our payment practices.

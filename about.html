Our Story

Easy Tape was founded in 2015, with an idea sparked by our founder, Elizabeth Craft, a professional tailor with more than 20 years in the industry. Elizabeth realized that many of her clients suffered from the discomfort of traditional rigid measuring tapes putting pressure on their skin.

Not finding anything that met her standards, Elizabeth went on a quest to write her own. Partnering with textile engineers and designers, she created a prototype for a soft measuring tape that wouldn’t sacrifice accuracy.

From Elizabeth's small workshop to today's well known brand serving professionals to DIY-ers. Today, Easy Tape is known for quality, precision and easy to use design.

Our Mission

At Easy Tape, we are on a mission of finding the perfect balance between comfort, accuracy and durability when it comes to creating measuring tools. We don't think you should have to sacrifice comfort for precision measurements.

We continued developing new innovative sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing processes. All of the Easy Tape products are designed for the user and the planet.

We aim to be the preferred option in the measuring tools market, spanning across professional tailoring, fitness tracking, DIY crafts — the list goes on! We deliver it through actively listening to our customers and continually optimizing our products to their needs.
